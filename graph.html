Euro-Atlantic Defence Intelligence Architecture

This visualization maps the interconnected web of defence intelligence entities across NATO, EU, and national agencies.

The architecture is deliberately redundant, not hierarchical, designed for resilience against hybrid threats such as Baltic Sea undersea sabotage.

National agencies provide core collection (SIGINT, HUMINT, GEOINT). Alliance bodies (NATO JISD, EU SIAC) fuse and assess. Cross-alliance flows remain constrained by classification barriers.
